About us

Tupperware has transformed Indian homes and lives of Indian women over the last two decades.
We have made choosing sustainable solutions easy and created business opportunities for women from all walks of life.
We have always led our business with a “people & planet first” approach, which has brought us continuous growth and success.
Our 3 million+ strong sales force in over 80 countries makes a compelling testimony of this.

Tupperware® Products

We bring to you an amazing range of kitchen and homeware solutions and first-time-ever Tupperware products.
From on-the-go solutions to space-saving utility products that make your work effortless and fun, you'll find them all and more.
